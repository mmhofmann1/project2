My Dog, Buddy

I have a twelve year old Cairn Terrier named buddy. Here's some information about him.

Bio

  • He is the same breed as Toto from the Wizard of Oz
  • He is a Good Boy
  • He used to have a light brown coat but now he has grey hairs since he's getting old

Interests

    He loves going on long walks and smelling everything. He likes to chase after animals, leaves, and people. He gets a big nice Treat when he is done with his walk. He takes naps often and is sleeping probably 75% of the day. He sleeps at the foot of my bed every night, but in the morning around 6am he comes up the bed towards me to snuggle. He loves to play tug of war with his toys.

Abilities, Accomplishments, and Admiration

    His small 15 pound body used to be able to jump 5 feet in the air but now that he is increasing in age, its probably only about 2 feet. He barks and growls at the TV whenever there is an animal (he actually knows the meow mix jingle by heart, so he doesn't even have to look at the TV to know there's a cat when he hears it).

    Whenever I come home from work or school he greets me at the door and expects rubs and pets. If you put your finger to your mouth and tell him to "Shh" he will bark at you. I have no clue why. He brought an injured baby bird inside one day. He just picked it up in his mouth, walked inside, and set him on the floor. This proves that if he really were to catch up to a rabbit or squirrel, he wouldnt do anything. I tell him often that he is beautiful and precious

    I love him and he deserves the world
